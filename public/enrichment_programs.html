Holy Spirit School students are given numerous opportunities for enrichment activity throughout the course of the school year. Students participate in P.E., Music, Art, Spanish and Computer Classes – all taught by dedicated and qualified instructors.

Students in grades 5 through 8 are provided the opportunity to play Flag Football, Volleyball, Basketball, and Cheerleading. Grades 4-8 may also participate in Band which is offered in the morning. Grades 3-4 have the opportunity, and are encouraged, to participate in Choir after school on Tuesdays. We also offer a Performing Arts Program which enthusiastically performs a musical production each year.

Some students also compete in the Diocesan sponsored Speech Contest and the Academic Decathlon. There are also Speech Contests sponsored by Justin Siena High School, as well as Essay and Art contests that students enjoy participating in throughout the year.
