Holy Spirit School offers an after school Extension program that is open from 7:00AM-7:45 AM and from dismissal until 6pm Monday-Friday on days when the students are in school. Parents can elect to sign their children up for part-time or full-time care each month. Part-time care is charged at an hourly rate.

While at Extension, students will be provided with a nutritious snack and will be given the opportunity to conduct arts and crafts projects, complete homework in a quiet area for study, and have time for free play while under the direct supervision of our Extension staff.
