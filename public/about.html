Our school is fully accredited through WCEA and WASC; our most recent study taking place in 2009, where we received a six-year accreditation. The reason for this success is the challenging curriculum that is offered to our students in our Kindergarten through Grade 8. While striving to develop Christian boys and girls, we work toward developing the whole child who will become loving, caring members of society. While striving for academic excellence, our students have regular classes in Art, Music, Spanish and Physical Education. Religious Education and religious practice are important parts of our school day. In addition to a challenging curriculum that is aligned with Diocesan and state standards, we also offer Extra-Curricular Band, Choir, Drama and Sports programs for our students. We pride ourselves in having a very stable staff at Holy Spirit School. Several generations of families have attended our school, and they witness to the constant improvement of our programs with each passing year. Our computer lab is fully equipped with the latest technological advances, so that our students are proficient in the most up-dated programs.

Our school offers Before-and-After School Care, with trained supervisors to assist students with homework and interesting activities to help students relax after their school day. We have a 5-day hot lunch program for all students who wish to participate. Come and join us for our Open House during Catholic Schools Week or at a time which is convenient for you. It will be our pleasure to show you around and familiarize you with our great school.

May God bless you on your journey,
Sr. “Liz “ Elizabeth Curtis C.H.F.
