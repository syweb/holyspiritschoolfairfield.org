Holy Spirit School does not discriminate on the basis of sex, race, color, or national/ethnic origin in admission policies or employment practices.

ACCEPTANCE PRIORITIES:

  • Siblings of currently enrolled students.
  • Catholics who are members of Holy Spirit Parish and neighboring Parishes. A member is defined as a person registered in the Parish, using the tithing envelopes and attending Mass regularly.
  • Transferring Catholics who become members of Holy Spirit or Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Parishes, whose children are currently attending Catholic school.
  • Participating members of other Catholic Parishes.
  • All others will be accepted if school has capacity for additional students.

GENERAL INFORMATION:

  • Applications will be accepted during the month of January.
  • A $40 non-refundable application fee is required for each student.
  • Kindergarten applicants must be 5 years old by November 1st of the year they enter the school.
  • The Pastor and Principal will conduct interviews with each family.
  • Applicants will be tested to determine academic placement.

PLEASE NOTE:
Applications will not be processed until all required documents and fees have been received by the school office. Applications remain on the waiting list through the school year for which the application was completed. If you wish to have the application remain in consideration for the following school year, an updated report card and immunization record must be submitted.

PARENT PARTICIPATION:

Every registered parent at Holy Spirit School is responsible for 40 hours of service. These hours can be earned in a variety of ways including, but not limited to: Room Parents, Library Assistants, Coaching, School Advisory Commission, Parent Group Board, Transportation, Fund Raising Committees, SCRIP, Work Parties, Hospitality, Publicity, Hot Lunch Program, Yard Supervision.

REGISTRATION FEES:

Registration fees are based on the number of parent participation hours completed each year. New parents will pay $200 per student.
The registration fee for new students is payable upon placement following the principal/parent interview.

PROFIT DOLLAR COMMITMENT:

Registered parents at Holy Spirit School are responsible for contributing $150 dollars each year by participating in various fundraisers. Parents have the option of paying $150 at the time of registration.

SCRIP COMMITMENT:

Registered parents at Holy Spirit School are responsible for contributing $200 profit each year by purchasing SCRIP. Parents are asked to agree to one of the following options:

  • I agree to purchase SCRIP during the school year by purchasing SCRIP and/or registering through E-SCRIP. I understand that my participation will generate a profit of approximately $200 for Holy Spirit School. If I do not meet the SCRIP profit total of $200, I agree to pay the balance of my required profit dollars.
  • I choose not to participate in the SCRIP program. In lieu of my participation, I will pay the $200 fee for non-SCRIP participation.
