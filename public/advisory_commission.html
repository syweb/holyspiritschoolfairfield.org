The Advisory Commission at Holy Spirit School is made up of members of the school and parish community that come together each month and act as the advisory group to the administration of the school.

Our Parent-Teacher Group is comprised of the Principal, a teacher representative, and elected members. The primary responsibility of the group is to organize fundraising events throughout the school year. The funds raised are used to supplement the school budget, and help Holy Spirit School to continue to offer quality education at a reasonable price.
